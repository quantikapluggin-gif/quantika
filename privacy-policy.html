Quantika - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Overview

Quantika ("the Plugin") is a construction budget management plugin for Autodesk Revit, developed by Quantika Software ("we", "us"). This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you activate a trial or license, we collect:

  • Email address — required for trial activation and license management
  • Name — optional, provided during activation
  • Machine identifier — a hashed, non-reversible identifier derived from your computer name and hardware configuration, used solely for license validation

We also collect anonymous usage telemetry:

  • Plugin version
  • License status (active, trial, expired)
  • Timestamp of usage

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT access, read, or transmit your Revit models or project files
  • We do NOT collect budget data, quantities, prices, or any project information
  • We do NOT collect passwords, financial information, or personal documents
  • We do NOT track your activity within the plugin

4. How We Use Your Information

  • License activation and validation
  • Trial period management
  • Customer support and communication
  • Usage statistics to improve the product

5. Data Storage

Your license information is stored locally on your computer in the application data folder. Usage telemetry is transmitted to our servers (Google Sheets) via encrypted HTTPS connections.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively for license management and product improvement.

7. Data Retention

We retain your activation data for as long as your license is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Security

All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption. Machine identifiers are stored as irreversible SHA-256 hashes. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of telemetry by disconnecting from the internet (the plugin works fully offline)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website.
